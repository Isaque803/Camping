Calanguas Islands Campsite is a unique camping destination located in close proximity to the beautiful Calanguas Islands. The campsite is set in a stunning natural environment, surrounded by lush greenery and crystal clear waters. The campsite offers a range of amenities, including tent sites, RV hookups, and a central community area with restrooms, showers, and a picnic area. The Calanguas Islands are a popular destination for snorkeling, diving, and other water sports, and the campsite is the perfect base for exploring the islands and enjoying the many activities they have to offer.
