Ulap Desert

$62.99/night

Ulap desert camping is a unique experience located in the heart of the desert. The campsite is surrounded by stunning sand dunes and breathtaking views. It is the perfect place to disconnect from the hustle and bustle of everyday life and connect with nature. The campsite features spacious tents equipped with comfortable beds and all the necessary amenities. There are also shared bathrooms and shower facilities available. Activities at the campsite include sandboarding, camel rides, and desert safaris. Guests can also enjoy stargazing at night and exploring the surrounding desert on foot or by bike.
