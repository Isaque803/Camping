Seven Sisters

Located in the breathtaking Norwegian countryside, this campsite sits just a short distance from a stunning waterfall. Whether you're looking to hike through the nearby forests, take a dip in the crystal-clear waters of the waterfall, or simply relax and unwind in the great outdoors, this campsite has something for everyone.
